The Dambulla Economic Centre, the main and largest distribution network establishment for vegetables of Sri Lanka, vey conveniently located in the middle of the country.

Nuwara Eliya Farms

Nuwara Eliya, recognized for its cool weather and fertile soil, is a prime vicinity for vegetable farming in Sri Lanka.

The place's farms produce a numerous array of exquisite veggies, which include carrots, leeks, radishes, and potatoes, which thrive within the temperate conditions.

Jaffna Agricultural Market

The Jaffna agricultural vegetable market, situated in the northern part of Sri Lanka, is a bustling center of trade renowned for its diverse and high-quality produce.

This vibrant market offers a wide range of vegetables, including locally famous varieties like Jaffna chilies, onions, and eggplants.
